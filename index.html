OUR DRESS CODE IS BLACK TIE OPTIONAL.

Just kidding.

We’re more concerned with people’s hearts than what they are wearing. Feel free to wear what you want, whether that’s a suit or shorts.

We know that visiting a new church can often be intimidating, so don’t worry, we won’t ask you to raise your hand as our guest or do anything that will embarrass or single you out in any way. Our band will play a few songs and each week’s message lasts for about 30 minutes.

If it’s your first Sunday we’d love to connect with you. Fill out this form and our team will be in touch!
